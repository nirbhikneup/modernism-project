## Project Overview
This website is a small digital exhibit exploring two WWI-era modernist responses:
1) rejection/disruption through bodily damage and moral accusation
2) healing through mourning, continuity, and fragile release

## Featured Works
- Otto Dix: *The War Cripples* (1920)
- Otto Dix: *Shock Troops Advance Under Gas* (ca. 1924)
- Wilfred Owen: “Dulce et Decorum Est” (1917–1918)
- Siegfried Sassoon: “Suicide in the Trenches” (1918)
- Käthe Kollwitz: *The Parents (Grieving Parents)* (1921–1932)
- W.B. Yeats: “The Wild Swans at Coole” (1917)
- Siegfried Sassoon: “Everyone Sang” (1919)
